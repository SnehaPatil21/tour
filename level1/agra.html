Agra

Agra –
A Heritage City of India

Agra is a city where love and history meet. Home to the timeless Taj Mahal, it welcomes travelers with majestic monuments, warm culture, and stories carved in marble. Every corner of Agra feels like a walk through history, making it a truly unforgettable destination..

Places to Visit

Taj Mahal
Bathed in soft white marble, the Taj Mahal rises like a timeless poem in stone. Built by Emperor Shah Jahan for his beloved Mumtaz Mahal, it stands as an eternal symbol of love and devotion. As the light changes, its beauty whispers different shades of emotion, making it not just a monument, but a feeling.

Agra Fort
Rising proudly in red sandstone, Agra Fort stands as a powerful echo of India’s royal past. Within its mighty walls live stories of emperors, courage, and dreams of empire. Overlooking the Yamuna, it blends strength with beauty, reminding us that history is not just remembered — it is felt.

Fatehpur Sikri
Carved in red stone and silence, Fatehpur Sikri feels like a city paused in time. Once filled with royal footsteps and whispered prayers, its grand halls now echo stories of faith, power, and dreams. Standing beneath the open sky, it reminds us that even abandoned places hold immortal beauty.
